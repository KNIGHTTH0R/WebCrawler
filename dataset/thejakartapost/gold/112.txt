Jakarta Governor Joko "Jokowi" Widodo said on Friday that he was ready to run for president on the Indonesian Democratic Party of Struggle (PDI-P) ticket.Jokowi said he had been given the mandate by PDI-P chairwoman Megawati Soekarnoputri."I've been given the mandate by PDI-P chairwoman Megawati Soekarnoputri to become the presidential candidate representing the PDI-P," Jokowi said during a blusukan (impromptu visit) to Rumah Pitung in Marunda, North Jakarta, on Friday."By saying Bismillah [in the name of God], I'm ready to carry out the instruction," Jokowi said at 2:49 p.m. as quoted by kompas.com.Then Jokowi kissed the national flag behind him.Reporters and local residents around him applauded in response."Alhamdulilah [thank God]," they said in unison.
