Australia flyhalf Quade Cooper kicked 17 points as the Queensland Reds continued their strong play at home for a 22-17 win over the Stormers in Super Rugby on Saturday.The Reds have lost only five of their past 34 matches in Brisbane.Cooper looked to have sealed the match over the Cape Town, South Africa-based Stormers with a dropped goal, quickly followed by his fifth successful penalty, for a 22-10 lead with 10 minutes left.But the Stormers, who have lost their past four matches and have failed to score more than 20 points in any match this season, replied with back rower Sikhumbuzo Notshe scoring from a driving maul to get the visitors a bonus point for finishing within seven points.
