President Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono, who is also the Democratic Party chairman, told party supporters in Semarang, Central Java during party campaign on Saturday of his administration’s success stories in the province.He cited the construction of the double track railroad linking Jakarta to Surabaya that is almost completed, as the latest success. He said the double track railroad would benefit many regions, including Semarang.He then highlighted the renovation of Ahmad Yani International airport and the building of Tidar State University in Magelang among his administration’s success stories in Central Java.Yudhoyono came to Semarang with his son Edy Bhaskoro Yudhoyono, who is also party secretary general, and his wife Kristiani Herawati Yudhoyono.
