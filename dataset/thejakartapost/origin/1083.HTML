Singapore Marriott Hotel Introduces Stay for Breakfast Promotion with SG$1 Buffet Breakfast

Entertainment | May 19, 2014

Stay at the iconic Singapore Marriott Hotel and enjoy a sumptuous buffet breakfast at SG$1!
SYDNEY, May 19, 2014 /PRNewswire/ — Indulge in an experiential stay at any of the well-appointed guest rooms, designed with savvy travelers in mind. Featuring a classic yet contemporary style, guest rooms are fitted with luxurious amenities such as two signature 1956 leather Eames Desk Chairs, Full-HD TV, large walk-in rain showers, as well as extended vanity areas.
Strategically located in the heart of Orchard Road, the 5-star luxury hotel is linked to at least 5 shopping malls via an underpass. Rain or shine, you can always enjoy world-class retail therapy at your doorstep. With the Orchard Mass Rapid Transit station situated right under the hotel, major attractions and the rest of the island are but a train ride away!
Enhance your stay with a lavish buffet breakfast at Marriott Cafe. The restaurant offers all-day generous buffet fare under the “Freshness Redefined” concept — whereby 10 professional chefs take centre stage at the open kitchen to serve up a highly-curated menu of quality dishes from the East and West. Signature breakfast items include bread and pastries freshly baked every day, and the Singapore Chilli Crab Omelette — breakfast with a local twist!
Promotion is valid from now till 31 July 2014, Mondays to Sundays. Make your reservations on Marriott.com/sindt and quote S4B as the promotional code. For more information, please call +65 6735 5800.
Visit Marriott International, Inc. (NYSE: MAR)( http://www.news.marriott.com/company-information.html ) for company information
