World Cup fever

The Jakarta Post | Fri, February 28 2014, 12:29 PM
World Cup fever: Indonesian singer Millane Fernandez (left) duets with US singer David Correy to sing the bilingual version of World Cup anthem titled “The World is Ours”, a song with heavy Brazilian sounds courtesy of legendary percussion group Monobloco. Correy will sing solo on the English version, while Fernandez will sing the Indonesian version titled “Dunia Kita”, which incorporates the sounds of angklung. (JP/PJ. Leo)
