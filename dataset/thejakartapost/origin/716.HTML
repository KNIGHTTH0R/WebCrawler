Wake up your sleeping photo in your smart phone!

January 15, 2014

Fujifilm announced to release instant printer for smart phone = “Instax Share SP-1” on 15th in Tokyo. It enable to print out photo which is sent from smart phone on to high quality film. It accelerate enjoyment of sharing printed photo between close friends.

Fujifilm analyze that currently smart phone user is 2 Billion in the world. It will rise to 6 Billion in 2020. 10.8 % of user has the experience to print out photo from smart phone. It will amount to 200 Million in the world and increase to 600 Million in 2020. Fujifilm has the confidence to develop huge market mainly 20’s and 30’s woman.

Fujifilm has 80 years long history started from film company for camera. Continuously brush up film and neighboring technology to propagate its industry field.

In 2010 Fujifilm released instant camera “Instax camera series”. It has pretty outward and easy to operate for every generation. As a result it recorded over 2 Million sales in 1 year attracting not only 20’s 30’s woman but also every generation from 10th to 70th.  After releasing in 2010 sales amount increase 30 % in every year.

Director of Imaging Business Division Mr. Masato Yamamoto explains that in spite of digital era instant camera which provides simultaneous enjoyment after taking photo is valuable for everyone. Already “Instax camera series” is very popular in Chana, Korea and Asia. It spreads to USA, Europe and Middle East.

Following to recording sales of instant camera, “Instax Share SP-1” for smart phone will attract 2 Billion smart phone users. Fujifilm recites “Wake up your sleeping photo in your smart phone!”
