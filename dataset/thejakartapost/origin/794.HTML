Toshiba Declared Strategy to Realize Perfect Healthy and Vigorous Society

February 24, 2014

Toshiba declared strategy to realize perfect healthy and vigorous society. On 20th February CEO of Toshiba Mr. Hisao Tanaka announced road map of healthcare business field. “More comfortable, More relieved, More happy” is basic concept in this field. To realize it Toshiba concentrates various technology which is piled in it rich experience. It is categorized to 4 fields.

Checking health condition of each individual to prospect future disease and prevent outbreak of disease. Move Band is a wearable device to check body condition ex. consumed calorie, walking distance. Toshiba will develop body condition censor to monitor cardiogram, pulse, temperature, any noise etc. in 2014. It will be minimized to the size of grain of rice to swallow. In the body that small sensor will check the condition of body 24 hours.

Development of top facility for medical treatment and diagnosis will enable early detecting of disease. Already Toshiba holds good share of MRI system and CAT scanner in the market. Toshiba will develop breath detector monitoring hangover of alcohol, fat, diabetes, asthma and so on. To realize early detecting of disease Toshiba concentrates to DNA examining kit.

Preparing to aged society Toshiba provides senior nursing service. Cloud computing technology enable sharing of body condition data of senior, connecting distant family to recognize condition of senior. Those technology will realize most relieved circumstance for senior and his family.

To maintain health in daily life Toshiba insists to air, water and food. Toshiba provides antivirus air conditioner including against technology to PM 2.5. To cultivate safe vegetable Toshiba progress to vegetable factory using control technology of temperature and humidity, CO2 producing technology and lighting technology to grow up plant.

In this field Toshiba prospects sales amount 5.9 Billion$ in 2014 business year and rise to 9.8 Billion$ in 2017.
