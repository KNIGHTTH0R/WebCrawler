Hotel becomes million-dollar art gallery

<< Previous   Next >>

The Asia Hotel Art Fair 2014 used the Marco Polo Hongkong Hotel last weekend to exhibit artworks. Prices on pieces displayed ranged from $200 to $1 million. A Diamante Galasso painting and Nerone postcards from Gallery Koo greeted customers in this corridor.
