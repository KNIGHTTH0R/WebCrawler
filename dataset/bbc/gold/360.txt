Media requires JavaScript to play.Bangalore and its nightclub scene have a had a tricky relationship over the last few years.In 2008, a curfew and dancing ban were put into place in Bangalore's nightclubs and bars. After protests and reconsideration by the police, the dancing ban was removed, but the 11.30pm curfew lived on.
